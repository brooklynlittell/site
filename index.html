About Me

Hello! My name is Brandon and I’m a Game Design & Development student at Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT). I strive to write software that leaves a positive impact on people through play or utility. I continuously push myself to work in new situations and environments so I can challenge my abilities and become more versatile.

My spare time is usually consumed by reading programming blogs and tech news, but I’m rarely hesitant to get away from the keyboard. I enjoy a variety of activities ranging from playing soccer to mixing music to amateur photography. My favorite break is when I grab a cup of coffee with a friend to discuss a coding problem.
